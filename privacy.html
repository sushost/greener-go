GREENER-GO Privacy & Licensing

Short version: no cookies, no third-party scripts, minimal server logs — and you’re free to reuse/modify this project with attribution.

Privacy

  • No cookies. We don’t set any.
  • No third-party requests. No external fonts, analytics, or pixels.
  • Server access logs only. Our web server records standard request metadata for reliability and abuse prevention. Logs are retained briefly and aggregated.

Why we collect it

To ensure the site works, defend against abuse, and understand basic usage. Legal basis: legitimate interests.

What we don’t do

  • No “selling” or “sharing” of personal data for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • No fingerprinting, adtech beacons, or profiling.
  • No storage of your search terms; they are handed directly to Google.

Where data lives

Our servers are hosted in the EU and powered by renewable-heavy providers (see Sustainable Hosting for details).

Your choices

If you have questions or requests, email us at support@sustainablehosting.com. We’ll help.
